Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 15, 2026

1. Introduction

HyPurDrive LLC ("we," "us," or "our") operates HyPurShot, a platform for auto detailing professionals. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our Service.

By using HyPurShot, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

Account Information

  • Email address and name provided during registration
  • Password (stored in hashed form)
  • Business name and profile details

Business and Media Data

  • Photos and videos you upload or capture through the Service
  • Captions and content generated or edited within the platform
  • Business information related to your detailing services

Social Media Tokens

  • OAuth access tokens obtained when you connect your TikTok account
  • These tokens allow us to post content to your social media accounts on your behalf

Customer Contact Information

  • Phone numbers of your customers, provided by you for the purpose of sending review request SMS messages

Usage Analytics

  • Device information, browser type, and operating system
  • Usage patterns, feature interactions, and session data
  • IP address and general location data

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide the Service: Manage your account, store and organize your media, and enable team collaboration
  • Post to Social Media: Publish content to TikTok and other connected platforms on your behalf using authorized OAuth tokens
  • Send SMS Review Requests: Deliver review request messages to your customers' phone numbers via Twilio
  • Generate AI Captions: Process your content through OpenAI to generate suggested captions and descriptions
  • Improve the Service: Analyze usage patterns to enhance features, fix issues, and develop new functionality
  • Communicate with You: Send account-related notifications, updates, and support responses

4. Third-Party Services

We work with the following third-party service providers to deliver our platform:

  • Supabase: Database hosting and user authentication. Your account data and application data are stored in Supabase's infrastructure with encryption at rest.
  • TikTok API: Used to publish content to your TikTok account. We access only the permissions you explicitly authorize via OAuth.
  • Twilio: SMS delivery service for sending customer review requests. Customer phone numbers are transmitted to Twilio solely for message delivery.
  • OpenAI: AI-powered caption generation. Content you submit for caption generation is sent to OpenAI's API for processing. We do not share personal account information with OpenAI.

Each third-party service operates under its own privacy policy and data practices. We encourage you to review their respective policies.

5. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

  • All data is encrypted at rest and in transit using industry-standard protocols
  • OAuth tokens for third-party services are stored securely with encryption
  • Access to production systems is restricted and logged
  • We conduct regular reviews of our security practices

While we take reasonable precautions, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Deletion: Request that we delete your account and associated personal data
  • Data Export: Request a portable copy of your data in a standard format
  • Correction: Request that we update or correct inaccurate information
  • Withdrawal of Consent: Revoke consent for specific data processing activities at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@hypurdrive.com. We will respond to your request within 30 days.

7. Cookies

HyPurShot uses minimal, session-based cookies only. These cookies are essential for maintaining your authenticated session and do not track you across other websites.

We do not use advertising cookies, analytics cookies from third-party tracking services, or any non-essential cookies.

8. Children's Privacy

HyPurShot is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected such information, we will take steps to delete it promptly.

If you believe that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us at privacy@hypurdrive.com.

9. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service. Upon account deletion, we will remove your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law or for legitimate business purposes such as resolving disputes.
