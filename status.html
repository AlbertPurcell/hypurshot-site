How we monitor

An automated health scan runs every Monday at 6:00 AM Eastern. It verifies that the site is reachable, the manifest and service worker still serve, DNS records for email authentication are intact, third-party SDKs are on supported versions, and that no required configuration value is missing. Failures notify the operator immediately.
