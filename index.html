Frequently asked

Quick answers

Do I need a Google Business Profile?

No. You can use HyPurShot to capture and showcase work without one. You just get more out of it when you connect one — real reviews on your public page.

Do my customers need to install the app?

No. They tap the link you send. Reviews go straight to your Google page from their browser.

Is there a contract?

No. Free plan is free forever. Paid plans are month-to-month. Cancel anytime.

What phones does it work on?

Any phone with a modern browser — iPhone or Android. Install it to your home screen if you want it to feel like a native app.

Do you charge per photo or per review?

No. Paid plans include unlimited photos, videos, and review requests. The free plan has small limits so you can try the whole workflow first.

I'm not a detailer — will it work for me?

Yes. If you send before-and-after photos to customers by hand today, HyPurShot works for you — painters, roofers, landscapers, pressure washers, handymen, and more.
