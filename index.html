How it works

Three steps from job site to five-star review.

A single mobile workflow replaces the three or four apps every detailer ends up bouncing between. Built for field crews, designed for repeat business.

01 — Capture

Shoot once, organize automatically.

Before-and-after photos, vertical video for short-form social, and customer notes are tagged to the job and the vehicle. No more digging through a camera roll at the end of a long day.

02 — Post

One publish button, every platform.

Direct Post to TikTok, Instagram, and Facebook with the right format, caption, and disclosures for each. Privacy controls, branded-content toggles, and music-usage acknowledgements are built into the form, not bolted on.

03 — Get Reviewed

Turn every job into a five-star review.

Automated, brand-safe review requests fire after the work is done — text, email, or both — and route customers to your Google Business profile, Nextdoor, and Facebook in one tap.

TikTok integration

Built on the TikTok Content Posting and Login Kit APIs.

HyPurShot uses TikTok's official APIs to authenticate creators, pull profile metadata, and publish video — with full creator control on every post. Tokens are stored server-side and revoked on disconnect or account deletion.

Operators can connect their TikTok account from Sign in and manage permissions on the team dashboard. We never post without explicit user action.

  • Login KitOAuth sign-in with the operator's TikTok account.
  • user.info.basicDisplay the connected creator's nickname and avatar.
  • user.info.profileShow the verified handle and profile link.
  • video.publishDirect Post a video the operator just composed.
  • video.uploadSave a video to the operator's TikTok drafts.
